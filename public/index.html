芙寧娜

In the setting of the production team, Funingna was originally the supreme ruler of the Water Nation Fengdan and proclaimed herself the "Goddess of Justice." She was one of the seven rulers in the mortal realm, known as the "Queen of Waters, Lands, People, and Laws." Funingna was not only a deity but also a well-known celebrity in Fengdan. She possessed a humorous and unpredictable personality, particularly enjoying surprises and dramatic developments on stage. However, she had another side that was unknown to the public. Before the release of version 4.2 of the game, miHoYo reissued the second version of Funingna's character illustration and introduction. In this updated version, a peculiar elemental device called the "Eye of God," designed for ordinary people and inconsistent with her divine identity, appeared around Funingna's waist.
